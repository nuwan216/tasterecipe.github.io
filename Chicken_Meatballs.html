Chicken Meatballs and Spaghetti
Recipe

Ingredients:

For Chicken Meatballs:

  • 1 pound ground chicken
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for cooking

For Tomato Sauce:

  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

For Serving:

  • Cooked spaghetti
  • Freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • Chopped fresh basil for garnish

Instructions:

Chicken Meatballs:

  1. In a large bowl, combine ground chicken, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, chopped parsley, egg, minced garlic, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.
  2. Shape the mixture into small meatballs, about 1 inch in diameter.
  3. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Cook the meatballs until browned on all sides and cooked through, about 8-10 minutes. Ensure they reach an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C).

Tomato Sauce:

  1. In a separate saucepan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  2. Pour in crushed tomatoes, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Stir well and let it simmer for 15-20 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.

Serving:

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Toss the cooked meatballs into the tomato sauce, coating them evenly.
  3. Serve the chicken meatballs and tomato sauce over a bed of cooked spaghetti.
  4. Garnish with freshly grated Parmesan cheese and chopped fresh basil.

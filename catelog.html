POL SAMBOLA ULUNDU VADAI (MEDU VADA)
Pol sambol is a traditional Sri Lankan condiment prepared with freshly grated coconut, chili pepper and red onion. It is most often used as an accompaniment with rice, string hoppers, hoppers, as well as other dishes. Ulundu vadai (medu vada) are spicy lentil fritters that are very popular in Sri Lanka and southern India. They are eaten as snacks or at breakfast, often served with sambar.

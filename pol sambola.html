POL SAMBOLA
Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup freshly grated coconut
  • 2-3 tablespoons red chili flakes (adjust to taste)
  • 1 small red onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 medium-sized tomato, finely chopped
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground mustard seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • 1-2 tablespoons lime juice

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Coconut:
    In a dry pan over medium heat, lightly toast the freshly grated coconut until it turns golden brown. Stir frequently to prevent burning. Once toasted, set it aside to cool.
  2. Grind the Coconut:
    In a blender or food processor, grind the toasted coconut to a coarse texture. It should not be too fine; some texture adds to the dish.
  3. Mix Ingredients:
    In a mixing bowl, combine the ground coconut with red chili flakes, chopped red onion, minced garlic, chopped green chilies, chopped tomato, curry leaves, ground black pepper, ground mustard seeds, and salt.
  4. Combine Well:
    Mix all the ingredients thoroughly until well combined. Adjust the salt and chili flakes according to your taste preferences.
  5. Add Lime Juice:
    Squeeze lime juice over the mixture and stir well. Lime juice adds a tangy flavor to the Pol Sambola.
  6. Rest and Serve:
    Allow the Pol Sambola to sit for at least 15-20 minutes before serving. This helps the flavors to meld.
  7. Serve:
    Serve Pol Sambola as a side dish with rice, bread, or traditional Sri Lankan dishes. It's particularly delicious with rice and curry.

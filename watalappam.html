Ingredients:

  • 1 cup jaggery, grated
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 cup evaporated milk
  • 1 cup cashew nuts, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup sultanas (golden raisins)
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Heat grated jaggery with water in a pan until it melts. Strain to remove impurities.
  2. In a mixing bowl, beat eggs and add coconut milk, evaporated milk, and melted jaggery. Mix well.
  3. Add chopped cashew nuts, sultanas, cardamom powder, and a pinch of salt. Mix until ingredients are evenly distributed.
  4. Pour the mixture into a heatproof dish and cover it with aluminum foil.
  5. Steam the mixture for about 30-40 minutes or until set.
  6. Allow it to cool and refrigerate before serving.
